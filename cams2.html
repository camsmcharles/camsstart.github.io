Cams Hill Online Classroom (CHOC)

The Cams Hill Online Classroom (CHOC) is based on Moodle, and is intended to present structured home learning and more complex homework tasks.

Once you've logged in here: https://canvas.camshill.com/login/index.php

you can click on the class you'd like to go into, check the current class on your timetable on the right hand of the screen, or scroll down on the right to see assignments due from all the classes you're enrolled in.

Check out the help videos and text below to begin getting the most out of CHOC.

Email

Cams Hill uses Outlook for Office 365 for email.

To login, go to https://portal.office.com  and put your username before @camshill.com - e.g. 25bloggsj@camshill.com

Edulink One

Edulink One brings together your achievements, behaviour, timetable and homework into one interface.

Login here: https://www.edulinkone.com

The school ID is 'Camshill' with no spaces.

Your login, as with the other services, is your network username and password, e.g. 25bloggsj

Getting Started

You'll need a Cams Hill network login to sign into the online services Cams offers. Usually, you'll have had this in your first IT lesson to sign into the computers, but if you don't have it, don't worry, just email onlinelearning@camshill.com with your full name, and we'll send it to you.

Watch the videos below to learn how to log into your email and CHOC, and get a brief overview of the courses you're in. The course continues on CHOC, so once you're inside the Online Learning course there, you'll find the next video in the series!

Mobile Apps

Because CHOC is based on the Moodle VLE, you can use the Moodle App, available in both the iOS App Store and the Android Play Store, to access Moodle. This makes it easy to upload photographs of work and access CHOC on a mobile device.

When you're asked to enter the site address, enter: https://canvas.camshill.com
