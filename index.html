Welcome. Above you'll find links to the main elements of our online learning platform, and to the left you'll find links to our tutorials on how to use them.
You'll need a Cams network login for all of these, so contact onlinelearning@camshill.com if you haven't been given one by your IT teacher yet.

CHOC

Is our online classroom, based on Moodle. Here, you can organise your home learning, submit work to teachers, and watch live and recorded lessons.

Edulink One

Here, you can check your achievements and behaviour points, your timetable, and any homework you've been set.

Office Portal

This can be used to check your email, use the Microsoft Office apps online, and access your files on OneDrive. You can also download Microsoft Office for free!
